Privacy

ChemVantage uses LTI to receive user and course information from your LMS for the purposes of authentication, delivering assessments, and returning grades. Data are retained only as long as necessary to support these functions.

  • Personal data shared via LTI depends on your LMS configuration and role.
  • Data are not shared with or sold to third parties.
  • No user Personal Identifiable Information (PII) is stored on ChemVantage servers
  • Instructors and administrators can request data removal as permitted by institutional policy.

See the detailed ChemVantage privacy policy.

Accessibility

This site is designed to meet WCAG 2.1 AA. We use semantic HTML, descriptive link text, labels for form fields, keyboard‑accessible controls, and high‑contrast colors.

  • Navigate by keyboard using Tab/Shift+Tab; focus states are always visible.
  • Images and icons include suitable alternative text or ARIA labels.
  • Content reflows for small screens without loss of information or functionality.

Please report any accessibility barriers to admin@chemvantage.org.

Common questions

How do students access ChemVantage?

Students launch ChemVantage from assignments inside the LMS course. No separate student account is required.

Which LMS platforms are supported?

ChemVantage works with LTI 1.3 Advantage-capable platforms including Canvas, Blackboard, Brightspace, Moodle, Sakai, and Schoology.

Can instructors create their own assignments?

Yes. Instructors can choose topics with Deep Linking, configure policies, customize items, and publish assignments to the course.

How does grading work?

ChemVantage automatically scores assignments and returns grades to the LMS gradebook through LTI Assignment and Grade Services.

How much does ChemVantage cost?

Instructor accounts are free. Individual student licenses are $8 per semester. Schools can buy annual student licenses in bulk for $5 each.

Is ChemVantage accessible and privacy aware?

The site is built with WCAG 2.1 AA practices and collects only the data needed to authenticate users, deliver assignments, and return grades.

Can we preview it before installing?

Yes. Use the sample assignments on this page, or request a meeting for a professor-focused walkthrough.
