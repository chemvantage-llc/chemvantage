1. Program Overview

The ChemVantage Referral Rewards Program ("Program") is designed to encourage current users and supporters to refer educational institutions to ChemVantage, an affordable high-quality LTI homework, quiz, and assignment application for General Chemistry.

By participating in this Program, you ("Referrer") agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions, as well as ChemVantage's general Terms and Conditions of Use and Privacy Policy.

2. Eligibility

To participate in the Program, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age or the age of majority in your jurisdiction

  • Not be an employee, contractor, or immediate family member of an employee or contractor of ChemVantage LLC

ChemVantage LLC reserves the right to verify eligibility and disqualify any participant at its sole discretion.

3. How the Program Works

3.1 Referral Process

Referrers will receive a unique referral link in the format: https://www.chemvantage.org/rewards/[unique-code]

This link must be shared with prospective institutions. When a representative from an educational institution follows your referral link and completes the activation process, the referral is recorded.

3.2 Qualifying Activation

A "Qualifying Activation" occurs when:

  • An educational institution that is not currently a ChemVantage client uses your referral link
  • The institution completes the account activation process, including email verification
  • ChemVantage LLC verifies the institution as legitimate and in good standing
  • The institution activates ChemVantage for use within their Learning Management System (LMS)

4. Rewards

4.1 Referrer Reward

Upon a Qualifying Activation, the first Referrer whose link was used will receive:

  • One (1) $50 USD Amazon.com gift card

4.2 Referred Institution Reward

The newly activated institution will receive:

  • ChemVantage service free of charge to the institution
  • All students from the institution receive a one-semester free trial subscription (regular price: $8 USD per student per semester)

4.3 Self-Referral

If your institution is not currently a ChemVantage client, you may use your own referral link to activate your institution. In this case, you qualify for both:

  • The $50 Amazon gift card (as Referrer)
  • Free institutional access and student trial subscriptions (as referred institution)

4.4 Limitations

  • One reward per activation: Only the first Referrer whose link leads to a Qualifying Activation will receive the reward
  • One reward per institution: Each educational institution may only be referred once under this Program
  • Maximum rewards per Referrer: No limit on the number of successful referrals per Referrer, provided each is for a different qualifying institution

5. Reward Fulfillment

5.1 Verification Period

ChemVantage LLC will verify Qualifying Activations within 30 business days of the activation date. During this period, we will:

  • Confirm the institution's identity and eligibility
  • Verify email addresses and contact information
  • Ensure compliance with these Terms and Conditions

5.2 Reward Delivery

Upon successful verification:

  • Amazon gift cards will be delivered via email to the address associated with the Referrer's account
  • Gift cards will be sent within 60 days of verification
  • Institutional benefits will be activated immediately upon verification
  • Student trial subscriptions will be available for the current or next academic semester

Referrers must maintain a valid email address on file. ChemVantage LLC is not responsible for rewards that cannot be delivered due to incorrect, outdated, or invalid contact information.
